How This Works

1. Choose your picnic

Choose a theme and any add ons you would like to be included in your experience then head on over to the booking page and fill out all the information required. We will respond to your message in 24 - 48 hours.

2. Pay initial deposit

Once the details of your picnic are all sorted out, you'll make your initial deposit of 50% and we start making the magic happen!

3. Enjoy the experience

Day of picnic, you show up to the location and everything will be ready for you to use and enjoy! We take care of setup and cleanup so you can sit and enjoy the beautiful views Maine has to offer.

Themes

Each experience starts at $250 for 2 people for 2 hours. Each theme is made to enhance all your 5 senses; taste, smell, hearing, touch, and sight. Included in each package is a complimentary sweet tray, bluetooth speaker, and games or cards depending on theme. You can add even more enhancements to your experience for the ultimate vibe at an additional cost (check out our add - ons).
