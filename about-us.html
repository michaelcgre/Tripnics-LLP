About Us

Tripnics was born out of central Maine in spring 2022. Our goal is to offer unforgettable, serene, scenic experiences for every client and their guests, without disrupting the natural area. We hope to bring zen energy and picture-perfect moments to various gatherings - birthdays, anniversaries, proposals, date night, photo shoots, etc. Let us take care of set up and clean up for you, all you have to do is be present.
