What is tripnics?

Tripnics LLP is all about taking picnics to the next level. We specialize in designing and executing extraordinary picnics that will make your guests feel like they've gone down the rabbit hole and entered a whole new world. Our picnics are thoughtfully designed and coordinated to make sure all your guests have the best experience possible. We’ll take care of all the details from catering to décor, so you can just sit back and enjoy the moment. Contact us today to learn more about how we can make your next gathering one to remember.

How This Works

1. Choose your picnic

Choose a theme and any add ons you would like to be included in your experience then head on over to the booking page and fill out all the information required. We will respond to your message in 24 - 48 hours.

2. Secure the date

Once the details of your picnic are all sorted out, you'll make your initial deposit of 50% and we start making the magic happen!

3. Enjoy the experience

Day of picnic, you show up to the location and everything will be ready for you to use and enjoy! We take care of setup and cleanup so you can sit and enjoy the beautiful views Maine has to offer.
